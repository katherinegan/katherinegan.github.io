Project Overview

    My Position:   Project Manager

    Team:   2 Project Managers (including myself) and 5 Strategy Consultants

    Duration:   December 2019 - May 2020

    Tools and Skills:   Microsoft Office Suite, Market Research, Primary Research


Indiegogo is a rewards-based startup crowdfunding platform, focused on supporting and raising brand awareness for entreprenuers primarly in the technology and hardware sector. Every week, there are thousands of new campaigns launched and innovative products introduced. My team was given the task of looking into the crowdfunding markets in the APAC region, and determining how Indiegogo can secure these new entreprenuers when expanding abroad.
