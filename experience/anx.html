AnX Robotics

Image processing and contrast enhancement for the Magnetically Controlled Robotic Capsule Endoscope.


Overview

    My Position:   Software Engineer Intern

    Team:   Manager, Project Supervisor, Intern Team

    Duration:   June 2020 - August 2020

    Tools and Skills:   Python, OpenCV, NumPy, scikit-learn, pix2pix


AnX Robotica Corporation is an advanced medical device company aimed at providing patient-friendly diagnose to analyze and diagnose medical conditions. This past summer, I worked on image processing for their Magnetically Controlled Capsule Endoscope within their NaviCamâ„¢ System. More specifically, the two main projects consisted of identifying the H value of color sensors taken from the capsule images, and improving the image quality through contrast enhacement.


Experience

Beginning the role with not too much experience with the type of work involved, I definitely had to learn quickly. Getting familiar with the libraries and tools available to me to begin writing python scripts and generate executables were some of the initial skills that I had to pick up on my own. Yet, I feel that I was able to become accustomed to the necessary libraries such as scikit-learn and OpenCV relaively quickly, and was able to put together the first project of identifying the H value of the color sensors in a GI image. After completing this project indiviudally, my team and I began working on the much larger project of image enhancement. The goal was to increase the quality of the GI images captured by the capsule through the training of an AI model. This involved researching different image enhancement algorithms, generating improved training sets, and writing scripts to help facilitate the image gathering process. Seeing the model improve with each new training set by calculating the PSNR and SSIM values of the images kept me extremely engaged and excited throughout the process, always hoping to make it even better.


Reflections

Working at AnX Robotica was an extremely unique and new experience for me, and offered me so many new skills that I believe will greatly assist my future academic and career aspirations. While I did feel overwhelmed at the start, I believe that pushed me to really take initiative on my own and pick up the skills I needed quickly. Overall, I feel that I was able to really grow as both a programmer and an individual learner, and had a great time working on my projects throughout the summer!
