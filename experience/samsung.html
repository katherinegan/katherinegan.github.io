Samsung

Examining a new developer platform to recommend attractive features to implement.

Project Overview

    My Position:   Strategy Consultant

    Team:   2 Project Managers and 5 Strategy Consultants (including myself)

    Duration:   September 2019 - December 2019

    Tools and Skills:   Microsoft Office Suite, Market Research, Primary Research


Samsung, a Fortune 500 technology company headquartered in Seoul, has their own platform for app developers. Here, developers of all stages are able to create and test their apps on Galaxy devices or the Remote Test Lab offered on the platform. Once finalized, these apps get published onto the Galaxy Store. My team was asked to identify how Samsung can grow its user base and create a more coherent experience for develpers, while also examining the effectiveness of gamification techniques.


Process

My team and I began by performing a competitor analysis with large corporations in the field to determine the market standards and compare this to what Samsung offered. We then analyzed the already existing developer website, and determined specific areas for improvement. For further insight, we gathered primary evidence through a survey given to students of all different backgrounds. Finally, we analyzed past attempts at gamification and its successes in various markets to determine its viability for Samsung when attracting new developers.

Being able to visit the Samsung office in San Jose to give our final deliverable was such a valuble and unforgettable experience, and I'm so happy with how our project turned out!
