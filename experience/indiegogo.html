Indiegogo

Analyzing international crowdfunding markets to best secure new entreprenuers.

Project Overview

    My Position:   Project Manager

    Team:   2 Project Managers (including myself) and 5 Strategy Consultants

    Duration:   December 2019 - May 2020

    Tools and Skills:   Microsoft Office Suite, Market Research, Primary Research


Indiegogo is a rewards-based startup crowdfunding platform, focused on supporting and raising brand awareness for entreprenuers primarly in the technology and hardware sector. Every week, there are thousands of new campaigns launched and innovative products introduced. My team was given the task of looking into specific international crowdfunding markets, and determining how Indiegogo can secure these new entreprenuers when expanding abroad.


Process

In order to be able to develop a feasible and appropriate strategy that to secure new entreprenuers in international regions, our team had to begin by researching in-depth the specific crowdfunding markets. This allowed us to develop a complete understanding of the particular situation facing each country and how these circumstances would impact expansion. From there, we were able to conduct some primary research to gain further insight on indiviudal company perspectives on crowdfunding. After gathering all of this information, my team and I were able to put together a strategy that we believed would help Indiegogo be sucessful in their expansion internationally.


Unfortunately, I am unable to share many of the details publicy, but please feel free to reach out with any further questions!


Reflections

This project really gave me one of the most meaningful experiences I've had thus far. I was able to get firsthand experience with so many new skills in various different settings. This included client sourcing and communication, effective yet flexible team leadership, and creating professional reports and slide decks. Working through the COVID-19 pandemic, I also had to adapt to a multitude of different schedules; I had team members all around the globe, including Dubai and Thailand! Overall, I was able to gain so much through this project and am definitely exteremely proud of how it turned out.
